## Curiosities
- Curiosities: "Diamante" is the fifth studio album by Brazilian pop superstar Anitta. The album features a mix of reggaeton, funk, and pop influences, showcasing Anitta's versatility as an artist. The lead single "Girl from Rio" was a commercial success, reaching the top of the charts in Brazil and several other countries. The album also features collaborations with artists like Mariah Angeliq, J Balvin, and Cardi B.
