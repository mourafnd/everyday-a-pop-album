## Curiosities
- Curiosities: The album "Em Ritmo de Festa" is the third album by the Brazilian duo Anavitória, known for their delicate lyrics and captivating melodies. With a soft and romantic pop sound, the record features collaborations with artists like Vitor Kley and Rubel, as well as a version of the song "Calendário" in collaboration with Ivete Sangalo. The album reflects the musical evolution of the artists and celebrates life and love.
