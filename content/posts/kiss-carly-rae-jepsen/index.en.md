## Curiosities
- Curiosities and any other relevant information about this album: "Kiss" is the second studio album by Canadian singer Carly Rae Jepsen. It received critical acclaim for its catchy pop hooks and Jepsen's unique vocal delivery. The lead single "Call Me Maybe" became a global phenomenon and helped catapult Jepsen to international fame. The album also features collaborations with artists like Owl City and Justin Bieber.
